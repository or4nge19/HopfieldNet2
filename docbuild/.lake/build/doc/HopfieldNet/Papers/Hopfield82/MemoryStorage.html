        def Hopfield82.isPseudoOrthogonal {R U : Type} [Field R] [LinearOrder R] [IsStrictOrderedRing R] [Fintype U] [Nonempty U] {m : ℕ} [DecidableEq R] [DecidableEq U] (ps : Fin m → (HopfieldNetwork R U).State) :
        Prop

        The pseudoOrthogonality property from Hopfield's paper (Equations 3-4) states: For random patterns, the dot product between different patterns is approximately 0, while the dot product of a pattern with itself is approximately N.

        This property is essential for understanding the storage capacity of Hopfield networks.
